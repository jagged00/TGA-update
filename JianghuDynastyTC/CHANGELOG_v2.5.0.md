# Jianghu Dynasty v2.5.0 - Great Schools and Sects

- Added eight original martial school archetypes.
- Added school rank flavor from unaffiliated wanderer through school master.
- Added school decisions for seeking, joining, training, guidance, rivalry, honor, teaching, inheritor recognition, and leaving.
- Added six original sect archetypes.
- Added sect rank and influence flavor from associate through leader candidate.
- Added sect decisions for contact, joining, influence, factions, reputation, alliance, rivals, elder support, and authority challenges.
- Added school and sect event pools with acceptance, rivalry, elder tests, suspicion, and reputation disputes.

All school and sect content is optional, player-triggered, and vanilla-map compatible.
