# Jianghu Dynasty Changelog

## v2.15.0 Final Vanilla Map Edition

Jianghu Dynasty v2.x is now feature-complete as a vanilla-map Song-era wuxia gameplay overhaul.

The final branch includes Jianghu reputation, martial schools, sects, Wulin gatherings, power struggles, social bonds, rivalries, court intrigue, frontier patriotism, military service, secret manuals, legendary arts, forbidden techniques, and final balance/cadence polish.

The unstable total conversion map, custom bookmarks, custom landed titles, province history, title history, and broad vanilla overrides remain archived and disabled.

Future custom Song map work belongs only in a separate v3.0.0 experimental branch/workspace.
