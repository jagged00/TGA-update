# Jianghu Dynasty: Free Life Martial & Military Sandbox

Jianghu Dynasty v2.15.0 is the Final Vanilla Map Edition for Crusader Kings III 1.19.*.

Build an original Song-era wuxia sandbox through optional Jianghu and Wulin gameplay: martial schools, sects, master-disciple ties, social bonds, rivalries, public gatherings, tournaments, secret manuals, legendary arts, frontier patriotism, military service, court intrigue, and court-Jianghu tension.

Features:

- Vanilla CK3 map gameplay overhaul.
- Song-era wuxia atmosphere.
- Original martial schools and sects.
- Jianghu social web and Wulin reputation.
- Master-disciple and honor/rivalry flavor.
- Secret manuals and risky martial techniques.
- Frontier patriotism and military career decisions.
- Court intrigue and mediator paths.
- No levels.
- No XP.
- No mandatory quests.
- No fixed ending.
- No custom map dependency.
- Compatible with CK3 1.19.*.

The full Song custom map is postponed to a separate future v3.0.0 experimental branch.
