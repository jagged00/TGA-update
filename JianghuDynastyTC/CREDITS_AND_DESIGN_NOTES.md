# Credits and Design Notes

Jianghu Dynasty v2.15.0 Final Vanilla Map Edition is an original Song-era wuxia-inspired gameplay overhaul for Crusader Kings III.

Design principles:

- Evoke Jianghu and Wulin atmosphere without copying copyrighted characters, places, dialogue, or plotlines.
- Keep the vanilla CK3 map active for stability.
- Prefer optional decisions and short event pools over mandatory quest chains.
- Use traits and modifiers for recognition, schools, sects, social bonds, and techniques instead of map titles.
- Avoid XP, levels, fixed endings, and class locks.
- Keep AI usage conservative and avoid global scans.
- Treat v2.15.0 as the final feature-complete vanilla-map edition.

The disabled total conversion map is preserved only as archived reference material.
