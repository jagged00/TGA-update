# Jianghu Dynasty v2.9.0 - Song Frontier and Patriotism

- Added Song-era frontier atmosphere while staying on the vanilla CK3 map.
- Added patriotism and independence modifiers such as realm loyalist, frontier protector, court-recognized hero, independent Wulin figure, suspected collaborator, and border veteran.
- Added frontier service decisions for defense, militia training, commander advice, supply escort, refugee protection, bandit hunting, and rallying martial heroes.
- Added court-Jianghu moral choice events for service, independent protection, and opportunism.

No custom Song titles are required.
